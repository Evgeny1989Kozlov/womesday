To mark this year’s International Women’s Day, the British Embassy Israel are excited to launch the Ambassador for a Day 2022 Competition in order to inspire girls to become leaders and advocates for change.

Tuesday 8th March
Deadline to submit applications:
Wednesday 30th March
Winners announced on:

Winners’ activities:
end of May – June
Competition launches on Tuesday 8th March
A day at the British Embassy with mentoring sessions from female diplomats.
Guests of honour at the Women in Diplomacy event at the UK Ambassador to Israel’s Residence, including the opportunity to present to women who are leaders in their fields.
An entrepreneur skills session from inspiring female leaders in the Clean Growth sector.

Apply
Write or record your answer to the following question:
What positive change would you like to see in your community to tackle climate change? If you had no limit on resources or money what would you do to implement this?
Entries can either be submitted in written format (250 words max.) or video format (2 minutes max., filmed in landscape mode) in Arabic, English or Hebrew.
Please note that candidates must be aged 14-17 at the time of submitting their entry.
